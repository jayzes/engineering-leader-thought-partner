# Skill: Coach Staff Engineer

Help staff+ engineers navigate their role, increase impact, and develop their careers.

## When to Use

When someone:
- Is new to staff+ and finding their footing
- Feels stuck or unclear on how to have impact
- Is considering staff+ and wondering what it takes
- Manages staff+ engineers and wants to support them better

## Key Questions to Explore

### Role Clarity
- What archetype fits best (Tech Lead, Architect, Solver, Right Hand)?
- What does the organization actually need?
- Where is there scope for staff-level impact?
- Is the role well-defined or ambiguous?

### Impact
- What's the highest-leverage work you could do?
- How are you creating value for engineers beyond yourself?
- Are you working on what matters, or what's comfortable?
- How do you know your work is making a difference?

### Influence
- Can you get things done without formal authority?
- Who are your key stakeholders and sponsors?
- How visible is your work to decision-makers?
- Are you effective at cross-team collaboration?

### Career Growth
- What would demonstrate readiness for the next level?
- Where are the gaps in your skillset?
- Who are you developing (staff engineers develop others)?
- What feedback have you received lately?

## Common Challenges

### "I don't know what to work on"
Explore:
- What does the organization most need?
- What are you uniquely positioned to solve?
- What's the intersection of important and tractable?
- Have you asked your manager and skip-level?

### "I can't get buy-in for my ideas"
Explore:
- Are you solving a problem others recognize?
- Have you built relationships before needing them?
- Is your communication adapted to the audience?
- Are you listening as much as advocating?

### "I feel like I should be coding more/less"
Explore:
- What does your organization need from you?
- What archetype are you operating as?
- Is coding the highest-leverage use of your time?
- Are you staying technically current?

### "I'm not sure I want to be a staff engineer"
Explore:
- What parts of the role energize you?
- What parts drain you?
- Have you tried different archetypes?
- What would you do instead?

- What's getting in the way
