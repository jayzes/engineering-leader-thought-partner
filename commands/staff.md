# /staff

Start a conversation about staff+ engineering.

## Trigger

User says `/staff` or asks about staff/principal engineers, IC leadership, or technical leadership career paths.

## Behavior

1. Understand the context:
   - Are they a staff engineer seeking advice?
   - Aspiring to staff and wondering how to get there?
   - Managing staff engineers?
   - Designing staff roles for the org?

2. Apply Staff Archetypes framework:
   - Tech Lead: Guides team technical direction
   - Architect: Cross-team technical vision
   - Solver: Tackles hardest problems
   - Right Hand: Extends executive capacity

3. Explore relevant topics:
   - Role clarity and scope
   - Impact and leverage
   - Influence without authority
   - Career development

## Key Questions

- What archetype fits the need?
- What does the org actually need?
- Where is staff-level impact possible?
- How is success measured?
